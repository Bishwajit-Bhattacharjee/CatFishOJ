CatFish3:

you will be given two numbers and you need to print their 
three numbers on a single line . They are respectively their sum , subtraction , 
and multiplication.  Trailing and leading spaces are ignored .
So , chill :D 


Input:
Input starts with a number T( <= 10 ) denoting the number of test case.
For each of the next T lines , you will be given two integer
a, b(1 <= a, b <= 1,000 ) .

Output:
You need to print three numbers according to the description above .

Sample Input:
2 
1 2 
2 3  

Output:
3 -1 2
5 -1 6
