CatFish
You will be given two strings and you need to concat them and
print the resulted string .Trailing and leading spaces are ignored .
So , chill :D 

Input :
Input starts with a number T( <= 10 ) denoting the number of test case.
For each of the next T lines , you will be given two Strings .
Lengths of the strings will not be more than 50 .

Output :
You need to concat the 2nd string to the 1st one .

Sample Input:
2
a b 
Rifat Sir 

Output:
ab
RifatSir
