Catfish

Welcome to the first problem .
Here , you will be given two numbers and you need to print their 
sum on a single line .Trailing and leading spaces are ignored .
So , chill :D 


Input :
Input starts with a number T( <= 10 ) denoting the number of test case.
For each of the next T lines , you will be given two integer
a, b(1 <= a, b <= 1,000,000,000 ) .


Output:
For each case print their sum on each line . See the samples to be clear .


Sample Intput:
2
10 12
12 9
Sample Output : 
22
21
